Statement of the Problem

  1. There is limited consolidated scientific information summarizing the health benefits of carrots and carrot pomace despite their wide consumption and nutritional importance.
  2. The bioactive compounds present in carrots and carrot pomace and their specific contributions to disease prevention and health promotion are not clearly synthesized in existing literature.
  3. Carrot pomace, a by-product of carrot processing, remains underutilized due to insufficient integration of research findings regarding its practical applications in food and health-related industries.
