Statement of the Problem

  1. How do the physico-chemical properties of hog waste organic fertilizer compare with those of soil used for pechay production?
  2. Are there significant differences in the growth performance and yield of pechay under different levels of hog waste organic fertilizer and commercial inorganic fertilizer?
  3. Which proportion of hog waste organic fertilizer produces growth results comparable to or better than commercial inorganic fertilizer?
