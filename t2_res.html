Results

The review revealed that carrots are rich in carotenoids, polyphenols, dietary fiber, vitamins, and minerals, which contribute to their antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, antidiabetic, antihypertensive, antimicrobial, and anticancer properties. The findings also showed that carrot pomace retains significant amounts of these bioactive compounds, making it a valuable functional ingredient. Incorporation of carrot pomace into food products was found to enhance nutritional value, particularly fiber content and antioxidant activity.
