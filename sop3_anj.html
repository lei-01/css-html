Statement of the Problem

  1. How does the presence of Lactobacillus in the vaginal and intestinal microbiota influence susceptibility to urogenital and gastrointestinal infections?
  2. Which Lactobacillus strains are most effective in preventing bacterial vaginosis, urinary tract infections, and gastroenteritis?
  3. What mechanisms enable probiotic Lactobacillus strains to inhibit pathogenic microorganisms?
  4. Can oral ingestion of probiotic Lactobacillus strains modify and improve vaginal microbial balance?
  5. What role can probiotic Lactobacillus strains play as alternatives or complements to pharmaceutical treatments?
